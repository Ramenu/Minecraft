-> Press Q to shift into or out of wireframe mode
-> WASD keys for movement
-> Pressing these keys will change the selected block:
    * Press 1: Grass block
    * Press 2: Dirt Block
    * Press 3: Cobblestone Block
    * Press 4: Stone Block
    * Press 5: Oak wood block
    * Press 6: Leaf block
    * Press 7: Sand block
    * Press 8: Cactus block

-> Press the right mouse button to place a block (this sometimes doesn't work depending on the angle)
-> Press the left mouse button to destroy a block
